08
Lead Capture System
How inquiries are captured and sent to your team

Every service page on the site has a purpose-built inquiry form. When a potential client fills it in and submits, three things happen immediately — the lead is saved, your team is notified, and no enquiry is ever missed.

All leads stored securely
Every enquiry — name, phone number, service requested, message, and which page they came from — is stored in a private database on the server. Your team can review all leads in one place at any time.
Instant notification — your choice of platform
The moment a form is submitted, an instant notification is sent to your team on whichever platform you prefer — Email, Telegram, or WhatsApp. You choose one, we set it up. Your team can call the prospect while they are still on the site.
Context with every lead
Each notification tells your team exactly what service the enquiry is about — MEP, pool construction, maintenance, and so on — so they are prepared for the call before they dial.
